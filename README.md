# 🌳 Tree Jump - Auto Running Platformer

A fast-paced, auto-running platformer game built with HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript. Jump over obstacles, collect coins, and reach the goal in this addictive arcade-style game!

## 🎮 Features

- **Auto-Running Gameplay** - The character moves automatically; you only need to jump
- **120+ Procedurally Generated Levels** - Each level gets progressively harder
- **10 Unlockable Characters** - 🌳 🦌 🐿️ 🦅 🦝 🐰 🦊 🐢 🦗 🦋
- **Beautiful Graphics**
  - Smooth animations and particle effects
  - Color-changing obstacles with gradients
  - Glowing coins with rotation animations
  - Pulsing goal with checkmark
  - Animated clouds scrolling across the sky
  - Character-specific accent colors
- **Progressive Difficulty** - Game speed increases with each level
- **Scoring System** - Collect coins for bonus points (+100 per coin)
- **Mobile Optimized** - Works perfectly on iPad, tablets, and mobile phones
- **Touch & Keyboard Support** - Play with mouse clicks, spacebar, or touch input
- **Auto-Save Progress** - Your progress is saved automatically

## 🚀 How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Select your character from the menu
3. Click "START GAME"
4. **TAP/CLICK to JUMP** over obstacles
5. **Collect coins** for bonus points
6. **Reach the ✓ flag** to complete the level and advance

### Controls

- **Desktop**: Click with mouse or press SPACEBAR to jump
- **Mobile/iPad**: Tap anywhere on the screen to jump

## 📊 Game Mechanics

- **Obstacles**: Avoid ground-based spiked blocks and flying circular obstacles
- **Coins**: Collect golden coins for +100 points each
- **Goal**: Reach the green flag with a checkmark to advance to the next level
- **Difficulty**: Game speed increases by ~10% every 30 levels
- **Particles**: Visual feedback with particle effects on jumps and coin collection

## 🎯 Tips for Success

1. **Timing is key** - Jump at the right moment to clear obstacles
2. **Collect coins** - They add to your score and show your efficiency
3. **Watch the gap** - Leave enough space between obstacles to land safely
4. **Practice makes perfect** - The difficulty ramps up gradually, so you can learn the rhythm
5. **Pick your favorite character** - Each character has a unique color accent
